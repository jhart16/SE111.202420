Thor: Love and Thunder

ABOUT CAST REVIEW

Review

Joshua Hart 2/5/2024

I thought it was a lot of fun to watch the movie Thor:Love and Thunder. I have great fun with Thor: Ragnarok. It's a great movie, in my opinion, and a pleasure to see. Thor:Love and Thunder on the other hand, has an even better aftertaste; my reflections following the experience seem more pleasent.

Compared to my expectations, I really had a lot more fun. Though it made me roll my eyes in certain instances, I felt that the comedy in most of the scenes about Thor and Jane's love story was extremely well done and gave enough of room to breathe.
